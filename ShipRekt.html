Ship Rekt

Ship Rekt is a game I worked on during my second year at Goldsmiths, University of London. It is the first project I worked on within a team. I was able to discuss ideas with other members and work through challenges which occured during the process of development with Git. I worked on part of the programming and UI.

 

Ship Rekt is a local multiplayer party game which players compete aganst each other to horde the most gold! The gold spawn on the two centre islands where there can also be upgrades for the players ship! The players may also steal the gold from other players encouraging ship-based combat!
