About Me

I’m a 23 year old games programmer. I started programming when I was 11 years old creating Minecraft mods, this inspired me to choose games development as a career.

I have been interested in games for as long as I remember. One of my earliest memories is playing Golden Eye on the N64! When I’m not working on making games I am playing them or watching videos of other people playing them online.
